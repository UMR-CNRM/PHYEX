6 ! Purpose: (*)
7 ! The fuction tiwmx_tab returns the wet bulb temperature, but also the
8 ! corresponding saturation specific humidity as an output parameter.
9 ! -------------------------------------------------------------------
10 ! Computation of wet bulb temperature.
11 ! TIW is found iteratively. Note that Q is used instead of WV.
12 ! Converges VERY quickly. EPS is the threshold value for
13 ! TIW(n)-TIW(n-1) at wich the iteration is interupted. (n is
14 ! iteration number).
15 ! Modified in July 1988 by Stefan Gollvik
16 ! Converted to new fortran standard in Dec. 2006 by Karl-Ivar Ivarsson.
17 
18 ! INTERFACE : the function is intended to be used everywhere.
19 ! INPUT arguments (arguments d'entree)
20 ! -----------------------------------------------------
21 ! P : pressure (Pa)
22 ! T : temperature (K)
23 ! QR : mixing ratio humidity (kg/Kg)
24 ! FICE : fraction of ice (0 to 1)
25 ! EPS : The value determines the accuracy of the output value. 0.1
26 ! : is suffient in most cases. Low value means high accuracy but
27 ! : also high computational cost.
28 
29 ! OUTPUT arguments (arguments d'sortie)
30 ! -----------------------------------------------------
31 ! RS : saturation mixing ratio (fice determines if it is over ice or water)
32 !
33 ! QRSN : saturation mixing ratio for the wet bulb temperature.
34 ! (Kg/Kg)
35 ! ( the function itself is the wet bulb temperature (K) )
36 
37 ! Work variables :
38 ! -----------------------------------------------------
39 ! f : temporary variable , temperature (K)
40 ! dfdt : temporary variable (K/K)
41 ! t2 : temperature used in iteration (K)
42 ! dt : temperature residual (K)
43 ! dqsdt : d(qsat)/d(T) in iteration (1/K)
44 ! b : (latent heat)/(heat capacity for dry air) (K)
45 ! iter : iteration number
46

77

80  tiwmx_tab = t2

83  zdesdt= desdti(tiwmx, t2)*fice + desdtw(tiwmx, t2)*(1.-fice)
84  IF(zes >= p*0.61)THEN ! Do not to compute when condensation
85  qrsn = 1. ! not possible and avoid mixing ratio > 1
86  tiwmx_tab = t2

92  ENDIF

104  ENDIF
