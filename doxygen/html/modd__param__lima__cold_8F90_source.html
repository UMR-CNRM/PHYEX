37 !
38 ! m(D) = XAx * D**XBx : Mass-MaxDim relationship
39 ! v(D) = XCx * D**XDx : Fallspeed-MaxDim relationship

42 ! XC1x : Shape parameter for deposition
43 !
44 ! and
45 !
46 ! XALPHAx, XNUx : Generalized GAMMA law
