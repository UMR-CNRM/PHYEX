33!
34! Declaration of microphysical constants, including the descriptive
35! parameters for the raindrop and the ice crystal habits, and the
36! parameters relevant of the dimensional distributions.
37!
38! m(D) = XAx * D**XBx : Mass-MaxDim relationship
39! v(D) = XCx * D**XDx : Fallspeed-MaxDim relationship
40! N(Lbda) = XCCx * Lbda**XCXx : NumberConc-Slopeparam relationship
41! XF0x, XF1x, XF2x : Ventilation factors
42! XC1x : Shape parameter for deposition
43!
44! and
45!
46! XALPHAx, XNUx : Generalized GAMMA law
47! Lbda = XLBx * (r_x*rho_dref)**XLBEXx : Slope parameter of the
