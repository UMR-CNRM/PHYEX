33! Pointer of fields in microphysic species arrays. Microphysics species are
34! usually counted as KRR=6 or KRR=7. The extra "zero" adress for potential
35! temperature is a trick to improve vectorization when all these fields needs
36! the same treatement.
37INTEGER, PARAMETER :: & ! pointer of fields in microphysic species arrays :
38 & ITH=0, & ! Potential temperature
39 & IRV=1, & ! Water vapor
40 & IRC=2, & ! Cloud water
41 & IRR=3, & ! Rain water
42 & IRI=4, & ! Pristine ice
43 & IRS=5, & ! Snow/aggregate
44 & IRG=6, & ! Graupel
45 & IRH=7 ! Hail
46!
47! Pointers for tendency arrays
48! Tendencies are computed either directly as a tendency or as a mixing ratio change that is transformed, afterwards, in a tendency
49! The second type is suffixed by _MR
50! Some final tendencies can have two contributions (one from a tendency, one from a mixing ratio change).
51! In the following list, order matters:
52! - first are the normal tendencies directly computed as tendencies
53! - second are the tendencies computed only from a mixing ratio change
54! - third are the indexes used to designate the mising ratio change part of double-contribution tendencies
55INTEGER, PARAMETER :: ibunum=47, & ! Total number
56 ibunum_mr=3, & ! Number of tendencies computed only from a mixing ratio change
57 ibunum_extra=2 ! Extra terms
58INTEGER, PARAMETER :: &
