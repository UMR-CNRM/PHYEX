24 !!**** * - compute the explicit microphysical sources
25 !!
26 !! PURPOSE
27 !! -------
28 !! The purpose of this routine is to compute the slow microphysical sources
29 !! which can be computed explicitly
30 !!
31 !!
32 !!** METHOD
33 !! ------
34 !! The autoconversion computation follows Kessler (1969).
35 !! The sedimentation rate is computed with a time spliting technique and
36 !! an upstream scheme, written as a difference of non-advective fluxes. This
37 !! source term is added to the future instant ( split-implicit process ).
38 !! The others microphysical processes are evaluated at the central instant
39 !! (split-explicit process ): autoconversion, accretion and rain evaporation.
40 !! These last 3 terms are bounded in order not to create negative values
41 !! for the water species at the future instant.
42 !!
