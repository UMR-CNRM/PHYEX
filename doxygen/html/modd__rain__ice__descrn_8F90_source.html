14 ! The purpose of this declarative module is to declare the microphysical
15 ! constants. This includes the descriptive parameters for the raindrop and
16 ! the ice crystal habits and the parameters relevant of the dimensional
17 ! distributions.
18 !
19 ! m(D) = XAx * D**XBx : Mass-MaxDim relationship
20 ! v(D) = XCx * D**XDx : Fallspeed-MaxDim relationship
21 ! N(Lbda) = XCCx * Lbda**XCXx : NumberConc-Slopeparam relationship
22 ! XF0x, XF1x, XF2x : Ventilation factors
23 ! XC1x : Shape parameter for deposition
24 !
25 ! and
26 !
27 ! XALPHAx, XNUx : Generalized GAMMA law
28 ! Lbda = XLBx * (r_x*rho_dref)**XLBEXx : Slope parameter of the
29 ! distribution law
30 !
31 !!
