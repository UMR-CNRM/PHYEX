29 !!**** *TURB_HOR* -routine to compute the source terms in the meso-NH
30 !! model equations due to the non-vertical turbulent fluxes.
31 !!
32 !! PURPOSE
33 !! -------
34 ! The purpose of this routine is to compute the non-vertical
35 ! turbulent fluxes of the evolutive variables and give back the
36 ! source terms to the main program.
37 !
38 !!** METHOD
39 !! ------
40 !! Complementary 3D calculations when running at high resolution;
41 !! The non-vertical turbulent fluxes are computed explicitly. The
42 !! contributions are cumulated in PRvarS and in DP and TP of TKE
43 !
44 ! d(rho*T) = -d(rho*u'T'/dxx) -d(-rho*u'T'*dzx/dxx/dzz)
45 ! / dt / dx /dz
46 !!
47 !!
48 !! Near the bottom of the model, uncentred evaluation of vertical
49 !! gradients are required because no field values are available under
50 !! the level where the gradient must be evaluated. In this case, the
51 !! gradient is computed with a second order accurate uncentred scheme
52 !! according to:
53 !!
54 !! D FF dzz3 (dzz3+dzz4)
55 !! ---- = - ----------------- FF(4) + ----------------- FF(3)
56 !! D z (dzz3+dzz4) dzz4 dzz3 dzz4
57 !!
58 !! dzz4 + 2 dzz3
59 !! - ----------------- FF(2)
60 !! (dzz3+dzz4) dzz3
61 !!
62 !! where the values are taken from:
63 !!
64 !! ----- FF(5)
65 !! |
66 !! | dzz5
67 !! |
68 !! ----- FF(4)
69 !! |
70 !! | dzz4
71 !! |
72 !! ----- FF(3)
73 !! |
74 !! | dzz3
75 !! |
76 !! ----- FF(2) , (D FF / DZ)
77 !! | dzz2 * 0.5
78 !! ----- ground
79 !!
80 !!
81 !!
82 !! EXTERNAL
83 !! --------
84 !!
85 !!
