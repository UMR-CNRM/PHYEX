28 !!
29 !! PURPOSE
30 !! -------
31 !! The purpose of this routine is to compute the slow microphysical sources
32 !! which can be computed explicitly
33 !!
34 !!
35 !!** METHOD
36 !! ------
37 !! The autoconversion computation follows Kessler (1969).
38 !! The sedimentation rate is computed with a time spliting technique and
39 !! an upstream scheme, written as a difference of non-advective fluxes. This
40 !! source term is added to the future instant ( split-implicit process ).
41 !! The others microphysical processes are evaluated at the central instant
42 !! (split-explicit process ): autoconversion, accretion and rain evaporation.
43 !! These last 3 terms are bounded in order not to create negative values
44 !! for the water species at the future instant.
45 !!
