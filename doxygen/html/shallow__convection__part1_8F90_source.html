17!!**** Monitor routine to compute all convective tendencies by calls
18!! of several subroutines.
19!!
20!!
21!! PURPOSE
22!! -------
23!! The purpose of this routine is to determine the convective
24!! tendencies. The routine first prepares all necessary grid-scale
25!! variables. The final convective tendencies are then computed by
26!! calls of different subroutines.
27!!
28!!
29!!** METHOD
30!! ------
31!! We start by selecting convective columns in the model domain through
32!! the call of routine TRIGGER_FUNCT. Then, we allocate memory for the
33!! convection updraft and downdraft variables and gather the grid scale
34!! variables in convective arrays.
35!! The updraft and downdraft computations are done level by level starting
36!! at the bottom and top of the domain, respectively.
37!! All computations are done on MNH thermodynamic levels. The depth
38!! of the current model layer k is defined by DP(k)=P(k-1)-P(k)
39!!
40!!
41!!
42!! EXTERNAL
43!! --------
44!! CONVECT_TRIGGER_SHAL
45!! CONVECT_SATMIXRATIO
46!! CONVECT_UPDRAFT_SHAL
47!! CONVECT_CONDENS
48!! CONVECT_MIXING_FUNCT
49!! CONVECT_CLOSURE_SHAL

51!! CONVECT_CLOSURE_ADJUST_SHAL
52!!
53!! IMPLICIT ARGUMENTS
54!! ------------------
55!! Module MODD_CST
56!! XG ! gravity constant
57!! XPI ! number Pi
58!! XP00 ! reference pressure
59!! XRD, XRV ! gaz constants for dry air and water vapor
60!! XCPD, XCPV ! specific heat for dry air and water vapor
61!! XRHOLW ! density of liquid water
62!! XALPW, XBETAW, XGAMW ! constants for water saturation pressure
63!! XTT ! triple point temperature
64!! XLVTT, XLSTT ! vaporization, sublimation heat constant
65!! XCL, XCI ! specific heat for liquid water and ice
